About

Me

I'm Jen! I live in Cardiff and grew up in Gloucestershire. I love exploring new places, and going to gigs and festivals. I like to keep active, mainly by going on walks and doing yoga. I also enjoy relaxing at home with a good book or Netflix series.

How I got into coding

My first experience of coding was in A Level ICT, where I learned some basic html. Years later I put some of that knowledge to use when I set up a blog through Blogspot, to document my study year in Malta. I quickly realised that I enjoyed playing around with the design much more than writing posts.

Then, in my final year of BSc Business Maths, my dissertation involved learning Python to create programs that calculated financial equations based on parameters inputted by the user. At first I didn't really get it, then one day it just clicked. Shortly after, a past graduate gave a lecture about his job as a software developer, which really resonated with me. It was the first time I understood how I could build a career out of my love for problem solving.
